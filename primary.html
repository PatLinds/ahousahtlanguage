Counting

lets count to 10 and beyond!

    Numbers 1-20

  • one - c̓awaak
  • two - ʔaƛa
  • three - qacc̓a
  • four - muu
  • five - suč̓a
  • six - n̓upu
  • seven - ʔaƛpu
  • eight - ʔaƛakʷał
  • nine - c̓awaakʷał
  • ten - ḥayu
  • eleven - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš c̓awaak
  • twelve - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛa
  • thirteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš qacc̓a
  • fourteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš muu
  • fifteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš suč̓a
  • sixteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš n̓upu
  • seventeen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛpu
  • eighteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • nineteen - ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš c̓awaakʷał
  • twenty - c̓aaqic

    • To carry on beyond ḥayu to 19, you would add "ʔuḥʔiš" to the tenth number - i.e. ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš c̓awaak = eleven.

    20 and Beyond

    To go beyond 20, you would say "c̓aaqic ʔuḥʔiš"
  • twenty one - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš c̓awaak
  • twenty two - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛa
  • twenty three - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš qacc̓a
  • twenty four - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš muu
  • twenty five - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš suč̓a
  • twenty six - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš n̓upu
  • twenty seven - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛpu
  • twenty eight - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • twenty nine - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš c̓awaakʷał
  • thirty - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu
  • thirty one - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis c̓awaak
  • thirty two - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis ʔaƛa
  • thirty three - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis qacc̓a
  • thirty four - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis muu
  • thirty five - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis suč̓a
  • thirty six - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis n̓upu
  • thirty seven - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis ʔaƛpu
  • thirty eight - c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis ʔaƛakʷał
  • thirty nine- c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔis c̓awaakʷał
  • forty - ʔaƛiiq


    • the "tenth" numbers are set to even numbers and doubled, so we would say
  • forty = ʔaƛiiq (2+2 in the tenth spot)
  • sixty = qacc̓iiq (3+3 in the tenth spot)
  • eighty = muyiiq (4+4 in the tenth spot)
  • one hundred = sučiiq (5+5 in the tenth spot)

    • we would then add "ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu" for the odd "tenth" numbers
  • thirty = c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu
  • fifty = ʔaƛiiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu
  • seventy = qacc̓iiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu
  • ninety = muyiiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu

    • then, we would add to that for the "ones"
  • thirty-one= c̓aqiic ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš c̓aawak
  • fifty-four = ʔaƛiiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš muu
  • seventy-nine = qacc̓iiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • ninety-five = muyiiq ʔuḥʔiš ḥayu ʔuḥʔiš suč̓a

Days of the Week and Months of the Year

there's sunday and there's monday, there's tuesday and there's wednesday, there's thursday and there's friday, and then there's saturday!🎶♪

    Days of the Week

  • Sunday - saanti
  • Monday - n̓upčiił
  • Tuesday - ʔaƛčiił
  • Wednesday - qacc̓učił
  • Thursday - muučiił
  • Friday - suč̓acił
  • Saturday - n̓upučił


    Months of the Year

  • January- yaqʷaḥuʔas
  • February - wiisaasumł
  • March - ʕaayaqumł
  • April - huaaqumł
  • May - ʕuuƛʕuuqumł
  • June - t̓aakƛaatasumł
  • July - ʔiicuuš
  • August - ʔiiḥ
  • September - naaʔał
  • October - č̓iyaaqumł
  • November - šiƛaatisumł
  • December - t̓iʕasumł

    Weather

  • sun - hupał
  • rain - m̓iƛaa
  • snow - kʷiis
  • hail - kacaa
  • fog - ʔučqak
  • thunder - t̓iicktiika
  • lightning - t̓iicktiikaʔiš
  • frost - k̓ʷimaac
  • ice - quux
  • wind - yuʔi
    • adding "ʔiš" to the end of these words will give it full sentence structure. hupał - sun, hupałʔiš "it is sunny."
