Primary Teachings

Counting

lets count to 10 and beyond!

Numbers 1-10

  • one - c̓awaak
  • two - ʔaƛa
  • three - qacc̓a
  • four - muu
  • five - suč̓a
  • six - n̓upu
  • seven - ʔaƛpu
  • eight - ʔaƛakʷał
  • nine - c̓awaakʷał
  • ten - ḥayu

11 and beyond

  • eleven - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaak
  • twelve - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛa
  • thirteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš qacc̓a
  • fourteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš muu
  • fifteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš suč̓a
  • sixteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš n̓upu
  • seventeen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛpu
  • eighteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • nineteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaakʷał
  • twenty - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓aaqic

    • To carry on beyond ḥayu to 19, you would add "ʔuḥʔiiš" to the tenth number - i.e. ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaak = eleven. To go beyond 20, you would say "c̓aaqic"

Days of the Week and Months of the Year

there's sunday and there's monday, there's tuesday and there's wednesday, there's thursday and there's friday, and then there's saturday!

Days of the Week

  • Sunday - saanti
  • Monday - n̓upčiił
  • Tuesday - ʔaƛčiił
  • Wednesday - qacc̓učił
  • Thursday - muučiił
  • Friday - suč̓acił
  • Saturday - n̓upučił


Months of the Year

  • January- yaqʷaḥuʔas
  • February - wiisaasumł
  • March - ʕaayaqumł
  • April - huaaqumł
  • May - ʕuuƛʕuuqumł
  • June - t̓aakƛaatasumł
  • July - ʔiicuuš
  • August - ʔiiḥ
  • September - naaʔał
  • October - č̓iyaaqumł
  • November - šiƛaatisumł
  • December - t̓iʕasumł
