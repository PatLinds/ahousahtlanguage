Counting

lets count to 10 and beyond!

    Numbers 1-20

  • one - c̓awaak
  • two - ʔaƛa
  • three - qacc̓a
  • four - muu
  • five - suč̓a
  • six - n̓upu
  • seven - ʔaƛpu
  • eight - ʔaƛakʷał
  • nine - c̓awaakʷał
  • ten - ḥayu
  • eleven - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaak
  • twelve - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛa
  • thirteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš qacc̓a
  • fourteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš muu
  • fifteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš suč̓a
  • sixteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš n̓upu
  • seventeen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛpu
  • eighteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • nineteen - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaakʷał
  • twenty - c̓aaqic

    • To carry on beyond ḥayu to 19, you would add "ʔuḥʔiiš" to the tenth number - i.e. ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓awaak = eleven.

    20 and Beyond

    To go beyond 20, you would say "c̓aaqic ʔuḥiiš"
  • twenty one - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš c̓awaak
  • twenty two - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛa
  • twenty three - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš qacc̓a
  • twenty four - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš muu
  • twenty five - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš suč̓a
  • twenty six - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš n̓upu
  • twenty seven - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛpu
  • twenty eight - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • twenty nine - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš c̓awaakʷał
  • thirty - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ḥayu
  • thirty one - c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis c̓awaak
  • thirty two - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis ʔaƛa
  • thirty three - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis qacc̓a
  • thirty four - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis muu
  • thirty five - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis suč̓a
  • thirty six - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis n̓upu
  • thirty seven - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis ʔaƛpu
  • thirty eight - ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis ʔaƛakʷał
  • thirty nine- ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiis c̓awaakʷał
  • forty - ʔaƛiiq


    • the "tenth" numbers are set to even numbers and doubled, so we would say
  • forty = ʔaƛiiq (2+2 in the tenth spot)
  • sixty = qacc̓iiq (3+3 in the tenth spot)
  • eighty = muyiiq (4+4 in the tenth spot)
  • one hundred = sučiiq (5+5 in the tenth spot)

    • we would then add "ʔuḥiiš ḥayu" for the odd "tenth" numbers
  • thirty = c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ḥayu
  • fifty = ʔaƛiiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu
  • seventy = qacc̓iiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu
  • ninety = muyiiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu

    • then, we would add to that for the "ones"
  • thirty-one= c̓aqiic ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiiš c̓aawak
  • fifty-four = ʔaƛiiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiiš muu
  • seventy-nine = qacc̓iiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiiš ʔaƛakʷał
  • ninety-five = muyiiq ʔuḥiiš ḥayu ʔuḥiiš suč̓a
