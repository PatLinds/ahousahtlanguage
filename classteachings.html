Classroom teachings - beginner sentences

there may be some repeats, but repetition is key in learning!

Morning sentences

ƛułʔiiš kuʔał - [it is a] good day OR good morning
ƛupkšiʔaƛi - wake up!
t̓ikpiʔaƛi - get up/get out of bed
t̓ikwiłac - I am out of bed
ƛułʔitk waʔič ? - did you have a good sleep?
haa, ƛułʔic waʔič - yes I had a good sleep OR wikyuus waʔič - no, I did not have a good sleep
ḥat̓iisč̓i - go have a bath
ḥat̓iis w̓it̓as - I am going to have a bath
m̓uč̓ičuʔaƛi - get dressed
m̓uč̓ičaƛs - I am dressed now

Maaqtusiis Cultural Ed - intermediate unit 3 audio2

t̓iqpiʔaƛ̓i - get up now!
čuu, t̓iqpiƛʔaqƛs - okay i̓ll get up
ʔuʔumḥiḥs yaaʔał pikčasc̓uy̓ak - can i watch tv?
ʔuʔumḥiʔick čaʔaałči - you may go ahead
wik, ƛ̓aaʔumʔasči čaani - no, go play outside for awhile
t̓iqʷaasiʔi - sit down
čuu, t̓iqʷaasiƛʔaqƛsiš - okay, i̓ll sit down
naʔaataḥʔis - listen to me
čuu, naʔaataḥʔaqƛsiš suutił - okay, i̓ll listen to you
huuxsʔatuʔaqƛḥin - shall we take a break?
huuxsʔatuʔin - lets take a break
waʔičuʔaƛ̓i - go to sleep
ƛułʔum waʔič - sleep well
ḥasiik̓aƛḥin - are we finished?
ḥasiik̓aƛniš - we are finished

Adult to Child pp week 2 pp

suw̓aaqk - is that you?
haa, siy̓aaqs - yes it̓s me
šaḥy̓utk - are you ok?
ḥaa šaḥy̓uts - yes i am okay
y̓uuqʷaak šaḥy̓ut - are you okay too?
ḥaa šaḥy̓uts - yes i am okay
šušuwaḥt̓inł - put your shoes on (shu shu wiḥ tinthl)
wiiwikḥt̓inʔuuƛi - take your shoes off
hasiik̓aƛniš - we are finished
šaḥy̓ut̓iʔaał - take care
